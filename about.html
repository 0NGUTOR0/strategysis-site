About Us

Who We Are

StrategySis Advisory Services Ltd. is a woman-owned and woman-led business advisory and management consulting firm delivering strategic insight, operational excellence, and transformative solutions to public, private, and social sector organizations.

We support institutions in navigating complexity, improving performance, strengthening governance, and building capabilities that drive sustainable impact.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s leading woman-led advisory firm empowering institutions to excel through strategy, innovation, and responsible leadership.

Our Mission

To provide evidence-based advisory and consulting services that enable our clients to make better decisions, deliver measurable results, and achieve long-term organizational success.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Our credibility is our primary currency.

Execution Excellence

Held to high standards to execute the best for our clientele.

Collaboration

Strong solutions come from win-win partnerships.

Our Differentiators

  • A woman-owned, woman-led firm bringing diversity, empathy, and innovation into the consulting ecosystem.
  • An in-depth understanding of Nigeria’s public sector, non-profit environment, and private sector realities.
  • A diverse, highly experienced Board of Advisors, deftly blending global best practice with local insight.
  • Strong network of subject-matter experts and strategic partners.
